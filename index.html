Quick Guide to Start with Trézor Wallet

Steps Description
Visit Trézor.io/Start Open the official website to begin your wallet setup journey.
Choose Your Model Select your Trézor device (Trézor One or Trézor Model T) based on your requirements.
Download Trézor Suite Install the Trézor Suite software for seamless management of your crypto wallet and funds.
Set Up Your Device Follow the instructions to initialize your wallet, create a PIN, and generate recovery seed.
Secure Your Recovery Seed Write down and store your 12–24 word recovery seed in a safe, offline location.
Add Cryptocurrencies Transfer your crypto assets to your wallet using the receiving address.
